Evaluation

• Luna presented with mild lethargy and decreased appetite over the past two days.
• Vitals were within normal range, but a mild fever (102.9°F) was noted.
• Abdominal palpation revealed slight sensitivity, suggesting possible gastrointestinal discomfort.
• No signs of external injury or parasites were observed during the physical exam.

Treatment

• Luna was given subcutaneous fluids to help with mild dehydration.
• A bland diet was recommended for the next 48 hours
• A probiotic supplement was administered to support gut health.
• The vet monitored Luna for 30 minutes post-treatment to ensure no adverse reactions.

Medication

• Metronidazole (Flagyl) : 50mg, twice daily for 5 days – to address suspected gastrointestinal infection.
• Famotidine (Pepcid) : 5mg, once daily for 3 days – to reduce stomach acid and ease discomfort.
